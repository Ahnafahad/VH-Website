# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LexiCore serves learners building a durable English vocabulary, including Bangladesh university-admission candidates and general English learners. They typically study in short, focused sessions on a phone and need the product to make the next useful action obvious.

## Product Purpose

LexiCore turns vocabulary study into a reliable daily practice through active recall, spaced repetition, adaptive review, and clear mastery feedback. Success means learners remember and use words—not merely collect points.

## Brand Personality

Scholarly, warm, and quietly playful. LexiCore should feel intelligent without becoming formal, childish, or theatrical.

## Anti-references

- A Duolingo imitation with mascot-led pressure or a large virtual economy.
- A generic analytics dashboard made from repeated cards.
- Decorative luxury treatments that slow study or reduce clarity.
- Shame-based streak messaging, fake urgency, or casino-like rewards.

## Design Principles

1. Put the next learning action first.
2. Make mistakes diagnostic and emotionally safe.
3. Reward demonstrated retention, not empty activity.
4. Let delight mark meaningful progress without interrupting flow.
5. Behave like a dependable app on every platform and connection.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Core journeys target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support zoom, large text, screen readers, keyboard navigation, reduced motion, color-independent states, 44px touch targets, and user-controlled sound and haptics.
